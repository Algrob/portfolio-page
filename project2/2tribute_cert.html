Kohei Uchimura

Kohei Uchimuras 7 year win streak

Kohei Uchimura is regarded by many as the greatest male gymnast of all time. He is widely known for his remarkable winning streak, capturing every major all-around title for a consequtive seven years. He was the first gymnast (male or female) to hold the all-around title for an entire single Olympic cycle, accomplishing this feat twice.

    Olympic Medals

  • G 2012 London All-Around
  • G 2016 Rio de Janeiro Team
  • G 2016 Rio de Janeiro All-Around
  • S 2008 Beijing Team
  • S 2008 Beijing All-Around
  • S 2012 London Team
  • S 2012 London Floor Exercise

    World Championship Titles

  • G 2009 London All-Around
  • G 2010 Rotterdam All-Around
  • G 2011 Tokyo All-Around
  • G 2011 Tokyo Floor Exercise
  • G 2013 Antwerp All-Around
  • G 2013 Antwerp All-Around
  • G 2014 Nanning All-Around
  • G 2015 Glasgow Team
  • G 2015 Glasgow All-Around
  • G 2015 Glasgow Horizontal bar
